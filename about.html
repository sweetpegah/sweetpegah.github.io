About Pegah

Pegah is a very talented pastry chef. Born in Iran, and trained professionally in Tehran and California, Pegah makes the most exquisite desserts and sweets for parties of all kinds. Pegah is very hard working and exceeds expectations. In addition to catering, Pegah also works as a restaurant pastry chef in Southern California.
