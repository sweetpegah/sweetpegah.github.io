About Pegah

Pegah is a very talented pastry chef. Born in Iran, and trained professionally in Iran and San Diego, Pegah makes the most exquisite deserts and sweets for parties of all kinds. Pegah is very hard working and exceeds expectations. In addition to catering Pegah also works as a restaurant pastry chef in San Diego.
